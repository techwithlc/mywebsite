Operation ENDGAME disrupted global ransomware infrastructure

Source: Securityaffairs.com

Operation ENDGAME successfully disrupted a global ransomware infrastructure. This operation included actions against the Silent Ransom Group, which was targeting law firms, and led to the arrest of 270 dark web vendors and buyers.

繁體中文摘要: 終局行動成功瓦解全球勒索軟體基礎設施。此行動包含針對 Silent Ransom Group 的行動，該組織以律師事務所為目標，並導致逮捕了 270 名暗網供應商和買家。

At Amazon, Some Coders Say Their Jobs Have Begun to Resemble Warehouse Work

Source: Biztoc.com

Software developers at Amazon are experiencing changes in their work due to the implementation of AI tools. Some report increased pressure to work faster with less time for thoughtful consideration, mirroring the pace of warehouse operations. Others welcome the shift, suggesting potential impacts on job roles and the nature of software development.

繁體中文摘要: 由於人工智能工具的實施，亞馬遜的軟件開發人員正在經歷他們工作的變化。一些人報告說，他們的工作壓力越來越大，需要在更短的時間內更快地工作，而沒有更多時間進行深思熟慮，這反映了倉庫作業的節奏。其他人則歡迎這種轉變，暗示了對工作角色和軟件開發性質的潛在影響。
