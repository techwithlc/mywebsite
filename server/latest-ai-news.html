Woman Alarmed When Date Uses ChatGPT to Psychologically Profile Her

Source: Futurism

A recent article highlights the unsettling trend of using AI, specifically ChatGPT, to psychologically profile individuals in dating scenarios. This raises ethical concerns about privacy and the potential for manipulation in personal relationships, adding a new layer of complexity to the already challenging world of dating.

繁體中文摘要: 一篇報導指出，在約會情境中使用 AI（尤其是 ChatGPT）來對個人進行心理側寫，這種令人不安的趨勢引起了關注。這引發了對隱私和在人際關係中被操縱的潛在道德問題，為本已充滿挑戰的約會世界增添了新的複雜性。

Wearable Tech: How Smart Devices Enhance eLearning

Source: Elearningindustry.com

Wearable technology is transforming eLearning by providing real-time feedback, hands-free learning capabilities, and immersive experiences. Smartwatches and AR headsets are highlighted as key devices revolutionizing how we learn, offering new avenues for engaging and personalized educational experiences.

繁體中文摘要: 穿戴式技術正在通過提供實時反饋、免提學習功能和沉浸式體驗來改變電子學習。智慧手錶和 AR 擴增實境頭戴設備被重點介紹為革新我們學習方式的關鍵設備，提供了新的參與和個性化教育體驗途徑。

Bill Gates Says AI Will Eliminate Most Jobs Except For Coders, Energy Experts, And Biologists

Source: Shtfplan.com

Bill Gates predicts that AI will displace a significant number of jobs, with the exception of roles in coding, energy expertise, and biology. This forecast has substantial implications for workforce development and the skills needed in the future job market.

繁體中文摘要: 比爾·蓋茨預測，人工智慧將取代大量工作崗位，但編碼、能源專業和生物學領域的職位除外。這項預測對勞動力發展和未來就業市場所需的技能產生了重大影響。

French State-Owned Bank Rolls Out $27,000,000 Initiative To Invest in Crypto Projects: Report

Source: The Daily Hodl

A state-backed bank in France is launching a $27 million initiative to invest in crypto projects. This move suggests a growing interest and acceptance of cryptocurrency within traditional financial institutions, potentially influencing the future of digital assets.

繁體中文摘要: 法國一家國有銀行正在啟動一項價值 2700 萬美元的計畫，以投資加密貨幣專案。此舉表明傳統金融機構對加密貨幣的興趣和接受程度不斷提高，這可能會影響數位資產的未來。

Has the Decline of Knowledge Worker Jobs Begun?

Source: Slashdot.org

The article discusses the potential decline of knowledge worker jobs, citing higher unemployment rates and slower wage growth for white-collar workers. This raises questions about the impact of automation and AI on the professional landscape and the need for workforce adaptation.
