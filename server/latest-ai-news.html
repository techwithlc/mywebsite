Obama: AI Better Than 70% of Coders, Gates Disagrees

Source: Windows Central

Former President Barack Obama suggests that AI is already surpassing the abilities of a significant percentage of human coders. However, Bill Gates counters this claim, arguing that the complexities of programming still prevent AI from entirely replacing human programmers. This divergence highlights the ongoing debate about AI's current capabilities and its potential impact on the job market.

繁體中文摘要: 前總統巴拉克·奧巴馬表示，AI已經超越了很大一部分人類程式設計師的能力。 然而，比爾·蓋茨反駁了這一說法，認為程式設計的複雜性仍然阻止了AI完全取代人類程式設計師。 這種差異突顯了關於AI當前能力及其對就業市場的潛在影響的持續爭論。

繁體中文摘要: ANNA VISION GROUP 正在巴厘島推出一個「智慧城市」，整合了用於城市開發的先進技術。 該專案標誌著AI和智慧基礎設施在房地產和城市規劃中的日益融合。 該計畫可能會為可持續和技術先進的城市生活樹立新的基準。

South Korea Data Center Market to Double by 2030

Source: GlobeNewswire

The South Korean data center market is projected to double by 2030, with significant investment and growth opportunities. This reflects the escalating demand for infrastructure to support AI-driven applications and the expansion of data-intensive technologies, such as autonomous systems and cloud computing.

繁體中文摘要: 韓國資料中心市場預計到2030年將翻一番，並有大量的投資和成長機會。 這反映了對支援 AI 驅動應用程式的基礎設施日益增長的需求，以及資料密集型技術（例如自主系統和雲端運算）的擴張。
