Microsoft, OpenAI to urge senators to speed power supply permitting, boost government data access

Source: The Times of India

Microsoft and OpenAI are advocating for streamlined permitting processes for AI energy needs and greater access to government data sets for AI training. This move highlights the increasing energy demands of AI and the importance of data availability for its advancement. This could accelerate AI development but also raises questions about infrastructure and data privacy.

繁體中文摘要：微軟和OpenAI正在倡導簡化AI能源需求的許可流程，並為AI培訓提供更多政府數據集的訪問權限。此舉突顯了AI日益增長的能源需求，以及數據可用性對其發展的重要性。這可能會加速AI的發展，但也引發了關於基礎設施和數據隱私的問題。

Researchers reboot push for AI safety after Paris summit bust

Source: The Times of India

Following setbacks at the Paris summit, AI safety researchers are refocusing efforts on mitigating risks associated with AI, including loss of human control and the potential for AI-enabled bioweapons. This renewed focus underscores the growing urgency of addressing the potential dangers of advanced AI systems.

繁體中文摘要：在巴黎峰會遭遇挫折後，AI安全研究人員正在重新努力減輕與AI相關的風險，包括人類控制權的喪失以及AI支持的生物武器的潛在威脅。 這種重新關注強調了解決先進AI系統潛在危險的日益緊迫性。

UOB and Accenture collaborate to transform customer experience using advanced technologies including GenAI

Source: Antaranews.com

UOB and Accenture are partnering to enhance customer experience using advanced technologies including generative AI. This collaboration aims to equip bank employees with new tools and training programs, showcasing the growing adoption of AI in the financial sector for improved customer service and operational efficiency.

US Man Shot Dead 4 Years Ago Addresses Killer In Courtroom Via AI

Source: NDTV News

An AI-generated simulation of a deceased man was used in court to address his killer. This case highlights the evolving role of AI in legal proceedings and raises ethical questions about the use of AI to represent individuals, especially in the context of posthumous communication.

繁體中文摘要：在法庭上使用AI生成的已故男子的模擬來與他的殺手交談。 此案突顯了AI在法律程序中不斷演變的角色，並引發了關於使用AI代表個人（尤其是在死後交流的背景下）的倫理問題。
