AI breakthrough allows doctors to 'see' dangerous blood clots forming

Source: Fox News

Scientists at the University of Tokyo have developed AI technology enabling non-invasive detection of blood clots. This breakthrough promises earlier diagnosis and intervention, potentially saving lives by identifying clots before they become life-threatening.

繁體中文摘要: 東京大學的科學家開發了 AI 技術，實現了非侵入性的血栓檢測。 這一突破有望實現早期診斷和干預，通過在血栓變成危及生命的威脅之前識別它們，從而可能挽救生命。

Why solar stocks have soared — and why there's trouble ahead for the rally

Source: Business Insider

Solar stocks have experienced a surge, partly due to policy support. However, challenges loom, including potential oversupply and the impact of AI-driven data center buildouts on energy consumption. The article explores the factors driving the rally and the risks ahead.

繁體中文摘要: 太陽能股價有所上漲，部分原因是政策支持。 然而，挑戰依然存在，包括潛在的供應過剩以及 AI 驅動的數據中心建設對能源消耗的影響。 這篇文章探討了推動反彈的因素以及未來的風險。

The US lawmakers raise concerns about Apple-Alibaba AI venture in China

Source: Neowin

U.S. lawmakers and government officials are expressing concerns regarding Apple's AI deal with Alibaba to bring AI features to iPhones in China. The focus is on data security, intellectual property, and the potential impact on U.S. technology leadership.

繁體中文摘要: 美國立法者和政府官員正在對蘋果與阿里巴巴合作，將 AI 功能引入中國 iPhone 的交易表示擔憂。 關注的重點是數據安全、知識產權以及對美國技術領先地位的潛在影響。

Coalition to unveil national semiconductor strategy

Source: The Irish Times

A coalition is set to unveil a national semiconductor strategy, offering generous subsidies to attract investment and foster growth in the Republic. This initiative aims to seed the next wave of technology-driven advancements and attract strategic investment.

繁體中文摘要: 一個聯盟將推出國家半導體戰略，提供慷慨的補貼以吸引投資並促進共和國的增長。 該舉措旨在為下一波技術驅動的進步播下種子，並吸引戰略投資。

Experts found rogue devices, including hidden cellular radios, in Chinese-made power inverters used worldwide

Source: Securityaffairs.com

Experts have discovered rogue devices, including hidden cellular radios, in Chinese-made power inverters used globally. This raises serious security concerns, particularly regarding potential vulnerabilities and data breaches.

繁體中文摘要: 專家們在全球使用的中國製造的電源逆變器中發現了流氓設備，包括隱藏的蜂窩無線電。 這引起了嚴重的安全問題，特別是關於潛在的漏洞和數據洩露。
