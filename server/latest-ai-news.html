Woman Alarmed When Date Uses ChatGPT to Psychologically Profile Her

Source: Futurism

A recent article highlights the increasingly strange ways AI is impacting dating. The article discusses a scenario where someone used ChatGPT to create a psychological profile of their date, raising concerns about privacy and the potential for manipulation in personal relationships. This points to a growing need for ethical considerations and guardrails in the use of AI within social contexts.

繁體中文摘要: 一篇最近的文章重點介紹了人工智慧以日益奇怪的方式影響約會。 文章討論了一種情況，即有人使用 ChatGPT 創建了約會對象的心理檔案，引發了人們對隱私以及在人際關係中被操縱的潛力的擔憂。 這表明在社交環境中使用人工智慧時，越來越需要考慮道德因素和護欄。

Wearable Tech: How Smart Devices Enhance eLearning

Source: Elearningindustry.com

Wearable technology, including smartwatches and augmented reality headsets, is transforming eLearning. The article focuses on how these devices provide real-time feedback, facilitate hands-free learning, and create immersive educational experiences. This signifies a shift towards more personalized and engaging learning environments, with potential benefits for accessibility and skill development.

繁體中文摘要: 穿戴式技術，包括智慧手錶和擴增實境耳機，正在改變電子學習。 這篇文章重點介紹了這些設備如何提供即時反饋、促進免提學習以及創造沉浸式教育體驗。 這標誌著朝著更加個性化和引人入勝的學習環境的轉變，這對可訪問性和技能發展具有潛在的好處。

Bill Gates Says AI Will Eliminate Most Jobs Except For Coders, Energy Experts, And Biologists

Source: Shtfplan.com

Bill Gates predicts that AI will automate a significant number of jobs, leaving coding, energy expertise, and biology as some of the few professions relatively unaffected. This prediction highlights the potential for widespread job displacement due to AI and raises questions about the future of work and the need for workforce retraining and adaptation to the changing economic landscape.

繁體中文摘要: 比爾·蓋茨預測，人工智慧將自動化大量工作，只留下編碼、能源專業知識和生物學等為數不多的相對不受影響的職業。 這一預測突出了由於人工智慧可能導致的大範圍失業，並提出了關於工作未來以及需要勞動力再培訓和適應不斷變化的經濟格局的問題。

French State-Owned Bank Rolls Out $27,000,000 Initiative To Invest in Crypto Projects: Report

Source: The Daily Hodl

A French state-owned bank is launching a substantial $27 million initiative to invest in cryptocurrency projects. This move signals increasing institutional interest in the crypto space and highlights the potential for blockchain technology to gain mainstream acceptance. This investment could drive further innovation and development within the crypto industry.

繁體中文摘要: 一家法國國有銀行正在啟動一項 2700 萬美元的重大計劃，以投資加密貨幣項目。 此舉表明機構對加密貨幣領域的興趣日益濃厚，並突出了區塊鏈技術獲得主流接受的潛力。 這項投資可能會推動加密貨幣行業內的進一步創新和發展。

Has the Decline of Knowledge Worker Jobs Begun?

Source: Slashdot.org

The New York Times reports that white-collar workers in the U.S. have faced higher unemployment and slower wage growth in recent years, prompting economists to speculate whether a decline in knowledge worker jobs has begun. This trend suggests that automation and other technological advancements could be significantly impacting the demand for traditional white-collar roles, necessitating an examination of the future skills needed to thrive in the evolving job market.

繁體中文摘要: 《紐約時報》報導稱，近年來，美國的白領工人面臨著更高的失業率和較慢的工資增長，促使經濟學家推測知識型工人工作的下降是否已經開始。 這種趨勢表明，自動化和其他技術進步可能會對傳統白領角色的需求產生重大影響，這就需要審視在不斷發展的就業市場中蓬勃發展所需的未來技能。
